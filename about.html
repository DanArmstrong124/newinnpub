About Us



General

This 18th Century Public House nestles cosily on the banks of the Wey Navigation Canal. With it's prime location and beautiful setting it is a favourite among pleasure boats, fishermen, walkers and dog lovers. Moorings are also available for canal boats alongside the beer garden.

The Team

We have an excellent team whos service can always be described as warm welcoming and friendly.

The Chefs

Our quality Chefs offer daily specials, traditional favorites and light meals, all of which are very generously portioned and homemade.

Our Ales

Our Real Ale selection is both varied and looked after to the highest standards, our range including, Hogsback T.E.A , London pride, Adnams and Doombar

Seasons

In the summer months the beer garden is a very picturesque spot for a lazy afternoon or a spot of lunch, with the winter months offering a cosy inside ambience with the two log fires and traditional decor, perfect for those cosy night out.

For lunch, dining, coffee or drinks, we aim to make your visit enjoyable and welcoming. Come along and try our seasonal menu

Whatever you come for, we'll be glad you did.
