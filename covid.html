Covid-19 Updates

Hello everybody and welcome back to the New Inn. Much has happened since we last opened our doors and the team have worked hard to learn the new guidelines to create a solution that we hope will work for everyone and make your experience a good one.


As many of you know, in order to protect our team and our guests we have opted to follow a gradual re-opening plan and initially will be operating outside only, with a reduced menu and hours whilst using the disposable crockery and cutlery that we use in our takeaway service. Although this may differ to other establishments, we believe this allows us to bring our team back together and learn new ways of doing things in the safest and most compliant way for us.


As well as signage and blackboards cross the grounds we have shared some ‘How It Works’ guides below showing our new way of working. As for many people this is all very different and we are learning every day so please bear with us as we may not always get it right and if you have any suggestions, ideas or just questions then we will be happy to hear them.


Finally a great big thank you to you all for your continued support it’s great to see you again and we hope you have a fantastic time.


Mark, Max and the Team
